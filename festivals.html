Festivals

Colorful celebrations and their significance.

Diwali

The festival of lights — symbolizes the victory of light over darkness and knowledge over ignorance.

Holi

The festival of colours — marks the arrival of spring and celebrates joy and togetherness.

Navratri / Durga Puja

Nine nights honouring feminine divinity — celebrated with dance, music and processions across regions.

Onam

Kerala's harvest festival with boat races, floral carpets and traditional cuisine.
