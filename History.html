Rich History

A curated timeline of India's most important eras and monuments.

Indus Valley Civilization (c. 3300–1300 BCE)

One of the world's earliest urban cultures. Noted for town planning, crafts, and trade.

Maurya & Gupta Empires

Major empires that advanced governance, trade, art and science across South Asia.

Mughal Era

Architecture and art flourished; many monuments like the Taj Mahal were built.

Colonial & Independence

Colonial rule reshaped the subcontinent; independence in 1947 began a modern nation-building era.
